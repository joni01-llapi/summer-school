Frequently Asked Questions

Still have questions? Call or message us anytime.

Who is this for?

Anyone aged 14–22 who wants to learn tech skills. Absolute beginners welcome — our Week 1 foundations track gets everyone up to speed. If you already know some coding, you'll skip ahead to advanced material.

What do I need?

A laptop (Windows, Mac, or Linux) and a stable internet connection. That's it. All software is free — we'll help you install everything on Day 1.

When are the live sessions?

Core sessions run Mon/Wed/Fri at 10am and 4pm EST. Pick the time that works for you. All sessions are recorded so you never miss anything.

Can I switch tracks?

Yes! Week 1 is all about exploration. Try different tracks, then lock in your choice by Friday. We want you in the right place.

Is there a refund policy?

100% refund if you cancel before Week 1 ends. No questions asked. After that, prorated refunds available.
